About Us

At Shawarma Kingdom, we’re not just serving food; we’re sharing a piece of our hometown, Newport, with every bite. Born and raised right here in this community, we know how important it is to have a place where you can enjoy delicious, high-quality food without breaking the bank. That’s why we set out to create a spot that feels like home and serves up mouthwatering shawarma with local flavor.

Our journey started with a deep love for the rich, savory flavors of shawarma—flavors that remind us of family, tradition, and the joy of good food. We wanted to bring those authentic tastes to Newport, but with a twist—affordable prices that everyone can enjoy. We’ve spent years perfecting our recipes, using the freshest ingredients, and sourcing quality meats and produce that reflect the values of our community.

Whether you're grabbing a quick bite after work or enjoying a meal with family and friends, we’re here to offer something familiar, comforting, and truly special. At Shawarma Kingdom, we believe that food is about more than just filling your stomach—it’s about bringing people together and celebrating what makes our town great.

From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the love, care, and tradition that go into every shawarma we serve. Welcome to Shawarma Kingdom, where every bite is a taste of Newport."
